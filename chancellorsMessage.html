Chancellors Message

Dr. José Agustín Lopéz Gónzalez Pacheco

Rector Magnificus (Chancellor)

Welcome to Universidad Azteca, Division of International Programs! If you are a prospective student for undergraduate, graduate, professional or continuing studies, I hope you will explore our Website to learn more about the international academic programs we offer, as well as the many services and activities we make available for our students. We are a university focused on the individual, offering each student a life plan that allows discovering human potential and how to focus for his/her benefits. We let students find their paths, giving meaning to their lives and conduct their work based on the principles of humanism and inspiration building their lives with the values of truth, justice and honesty.

The University offers the means to carry out a comprehensive education; we promote a transcending daily learning, providing tools to empower your professional and personal life. With advances in technology drawing the world closer and closer together, international students play an increasingly vital part in the internationalization of the academic mission of our Universidad Azteca, International students enrich the learning experience by allowing all of us – students, faculty, staff and administrators – to interact with people from diverse cultural backgrounds. I hope our Website and this brief message provided you with a glimpse of what the Universidad Azteca can offer international students. We are a dynamic institution that welcomes to international community of diverse backgrounds in the belief that their respectful interaction advances the discovery of truth, mutual understanding, self-realization and the common good. I encourage you to learn more about us. Talk to one of our students. Chat with a professor or Department Director & Chair. Explore our Web site. Find out how Universidad Azteca International Network System is helping others and how we can help you grow, achieve and succeed.
